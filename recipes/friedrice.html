Fried Rice Recipe

How to Make Delicious Fried Rice

Fried rice is a quick and delicious dish that can be made with leftover rice. It's a versatile recipe where you can add vegetables, eggs, or even your favorite proteins like chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast-1 cup
  • Cooked rice-2-3 cups
  • Eggs-2
  • Vegetables
    • Peas-1/2 cup
    • Carrots-1/2 cup
    • Green onions-2-3 stalks
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic-2 cloves
  • Salt & pepper
  • Vegetable oil-2 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Prepare chicken
  3. Cook chicken
  4. Scramble eggs
  5. Finish
